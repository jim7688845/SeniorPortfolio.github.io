The Holy Grail

The PC Masters Holy Grail line is built to handle the workloads of Computer professionals. This computer pumps out a lot of performance without a huge price tag. It uses Intel’s Xeon E5 V3 processors, which feature up to 18 cores and support for up to 512GB of ECC memory. There is also plenty of expansion room, allowing for various add-on cards to suit the needs of different workloads, as well as an optional quiet package which upgrades the fans and changes the cooling layout to minimize noise.
