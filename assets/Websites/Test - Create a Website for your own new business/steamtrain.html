Steam Train

 

 

The PC Masters Steam Train line of computers is built for gaming, and offers overclocking options. This is the standard Steam Train model, using the Fractal Design Arc Midi R2 chassis and offering liquid-cooling for the CPU. It is that cooling setup which provides the capability needed for us to overclock the processor upon request. It also features high-performance video card options, including SLI and Crossfire, and has more expansion room internally for add-on cards and additional drives.
